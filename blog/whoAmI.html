Who am I?

Sprint 1, April 2022

Gracie here, I'm based in Christchurch. Integrity, authenticity and balance are essential to me. It's so true what they say about 'first taking care of yourself, before trying to help others', to make sure you have a good life balance. But also 'treat others, how you would like to be treated' and 'always trying to put your best foot forward' and naturally 'learn from your mistakes'. Could I have included any more cheesy cliches? I'm attending Dev Academy because I love to help others, I enjoy the challenge of problem-solving, and I love dabbling in tech. Through gaining the right skills during Foundations + Bootcamp, I know I'll feel ready to hit the ground running, find an opportunity and join a team where I can make a difference.
